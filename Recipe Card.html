Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A classic and simple Italian dish of garlic, olive oil, and parsley. It's quick, delicious, and a perfect weeknight meal.

Prep Time 10 mins
Cook Time 15 mins
Servings 2 people

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (or to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Just before draining, reserve about 1 cup of the starchy pasta water.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add the sliced garlic and cook slowly until it turns golden brown. Be careful not to burn it.
  3. Add the red pepper flakes to the oil and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it directly to the skillet with the garlic and oil. Toss to combine.
  5. Add about 1/2 cup of the reserved pasta water to the skillet. Toss continuously for a minute to create a creamy sauce that coats the pasta.
  6. Remove from heat, stir in the fresh parsley, and season with salt. Serve immediately.
